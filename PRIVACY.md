AfterCredits Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how AfterCredits handles account information, app activity, and content you submit while using the app.

2. Information You Provide

Depending on how you use the app, AfterCredits may process information you provide directly, including:
- account credentials such as email address
- profile details such as display name, callsign, and avatar
- reviews, ratings, watchlists, status updates, notes, and other content you choose to submit
- settings and preferences saved in the app

3. Information Processed To Run The App

AfterCredits may process technical and usage-related information required to operate core features, including:
- authentication state
- profile and preference records
- media status records such as playing, completed, or wishlist entries
- app content you create, such as reviews or posts

4. Third-Party Data Providers

The app may request catalog, metadata, or media-related information from third-party services, including AniList, TMDB, RAWG, IGDB, Twitch, Supabase, RSS providers, and app-store infrastructure. Those services may process data according to their own privacy terms.

5. Local Storage

AfterCredits may store app data locally on your device for performance, caching, personalization, sign-in continuity, and settings retention.

6. How Information Is Used

Information processed by AfterCredits may be used to:
- create and manage your account
- personalize the app experience
- save your media progress, lists, and reviews
- load content faster through local caching
- support app stability, moderation, and product maintenance

7. Sharing

AfterCredits does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app, comply with law, enforce terms, or interact with the third-party services that power app features.

8. Your Choices

You may control certain profile and privacy settings inside the app, including anonymous display behavior where available. You may also stop using the app or request account-related support through the publisher contact channel listed on the official store listing or support page.

9. Data Retention

Data may be retained for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain app integrity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

10. Children

AfterCredits is not intended for use by children in violation of applicable age restrictions or platform requirements.

11. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after an update means the revised policy will apply going forward, subject to applicable law.

12. Contact

Privacy-related questions or requests should be directed through the publisher contact channel listed on the official store listing or official support page for AfterCredits.
